Guide to the Realm of the Existential Octopus:

Eight limbs grasp eight directions, clinging tight. Each limb leads to that which is held; each way is not straight. They wind and twist amongst each other. Five are explained below, one is presently explained, one is hidden beneath an octopus, and one leads elsewhere, yet appears in multitude.

=||□||=

Realm of the Talking Birds

In the realm of the Twitter, there exists disembodied birds with the voices of humans, tweeting away in an invisible realm that floats through matter, riding the air itself.

!<<☠>>!

Land with a Tower

In the realm of the Tarot, there exists a barren and dry land. No plants disturb the cracked soil, no birds chirp, no wind blows. The realm is unperterbed, save for a spiral tower that twists upwards for a hundred meters. The tower crackles with electricity as if it has sucked the energy from all corners. It waits for the moment to explode.

!!⏄∞⏄!!

Space of the Forever Twilight

There exists a practice of gleaning events which have not come to pass from a particularly potent dream. The dreamer and interpreter both inhabit a space between realms, where it is not quite dark, yet not quite day. Every deliniation is blurred. Past and future, awake and dreaming, abstract and concrete, concepts and matter, dreamer and dreamed of, between any objects themselves, all fluidly pass into and out of each other.

((//∆\\))

Plane of Chaos

Garbled symbols characterize this plane, stirring ripples across those ordered realms. The plane of action and energy, of the concentrated, the random realm creates and is created, generates and is a generation. For without the power dreamed from this realm, all would solidify into the utterly stable and unchanging. Amidst the plane of chaos dwells a sacred octopus, flying to and fro across the regions. It hides a key. Watch for this guide. It can help you find that which is hidden.
